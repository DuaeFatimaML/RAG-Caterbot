--- SERVING SIZE RULES ---
 
RULE A — GRAVY & RICE dishes:
  5 kg serves 10 to 15 people.
  Use 5 kg = 12 people as the standard for planning.
  Applies to: all biryanis, qormas, handis, pulaos,
  daal chawal, plain rice, egg fried rice, kheer,
  all halwas.
 
RULE B — DRY / BBQ / CONTINENTAL dishes:
  5 kg serves 7 people.
  Applies to: chicken BBQ, beef BBQ, mutton BBQ,
  chicken steak, beef steak, parmesan chicken,
  stuffed chicken, honey garlic chicken,
  kung pao chicken, chicken shashlik,gulab jamun,Besan laddu
